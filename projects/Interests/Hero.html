SURYA (South Indian Actor)

Surya is one of the most admired actors in Indian cinema, known for his versatile acting and dedicaton to his craft. He has started in many films that explore social issues, family stories, and action packed themes. Beyond acting, surya is respected for his humility and his work in educaton through the Agaram Foundation, which helps underpriviliged children gain access to learning opportunities. His commitment both on and off screen makes him a true inspiration to fans around the world.
