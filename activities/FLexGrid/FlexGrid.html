About Lions

Lions are majestic wild cats known for their golden manes and strong bonds. They live in groups called prides and are often called the "King of the Jungle".

The Lion

The lion is one of the largest cats in the world. It symbolize courage, leadership, and strength.

Habitat

Lions prefer grassy plains and open woodlands. They hunt mostly at night and rest under trees during the day.

Diet

Lions are carnivore that mainlt hunt antelope, zebra and buffalo. They rely on teamwork within their pride to catch prey.

Behavior

Lins are social animals that ;live in prides. The females do most of the hunting, while makes defend the territory.

Population

The lion population has decreased due to habitat loss and poaching. Conservation efforts are helping them recover.

Interesting Facts

A lion's roar can be heard up to five miles away! They can sleep up to 20hrs a day to save energy.
